Jurisdiction Selection Clause Template - European Union

Use Case:
Commercial agreements where an EU member-state governing law and venue are selected by contract.

Template:
Governing Law and Jurisdiction.
This Agreement is governed by the laws of [Member State], excluding its conflict of law rules. Subject to the arbitration clause (if selected), the courts of [City, Member State] have exclusive jurisdiction over any dispute arising out of or in connection with this Agreement.

Optional Arbitration Variant:
Any dispute, controversy, or claim arising out of or in connection with this Agreement will be finally resolved by arbitration under the [Arbitration Rules], with seat in [City, Member State], in the English language unless otherwise agreed in writing.
