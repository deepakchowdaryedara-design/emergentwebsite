# Legal Risk Dashboard (Board Summary)

**Document Type:** Governance Summary  
**Scope:** Privacy Policy, Terms and Conditions, and Legal Template Framework  
**Effective Date:** May 2026  
**Last Updated:** May 2026  
**Review Owner:** Legal and Compliance Function  

## Executive Summary

The current legal framework provides a strong company-protective baseline for cross-border service delivery. Core controls are in place across liability limitation, intellectual property ownership, compliance handling, data governance, and dispute preparedness. Residual risk remains primarily jurisdictional and contract-specific, requiring signed agreement alignment and counsel validation in each material market.

## Domain Detail

### 1) Operational Risk — Green

**Current controls**
- Verification-gated rights handling and lawful request limitation.
- Service suspension rights for non-payment, fraud, and security events.
- Client dependency language supporting schedule and cost adjustment.

**Residual risk**
- High-volume regulatory requests and multi-jurisdiction response complexity.

**Next enhancement**
- Implement standardized rights-request SLA and escalation matrix by jurisdiction.

### 2) Financial Risk — Green

**Current controls**
- Aggregate liability cap fallback tied to fees paid where no signed cap exists.
- Exclusion of indirect and consequential damages (subject to applicable law).
- Payment-enforcement rights allowing suspension and service limitation.

**Residual risk**
- Local law carve-outs in jurisdictions with mandatory consumer or sector protections.

**Next enhancement**
- Add jurisdiction-specific liability carve-out annexes for regulated sectors.

### 3) Regulatory / Compliance Risk — Amber

**Current controls**
- Region-aware privacy rights language and cross-border applicability framing.
- Company discretion to update policies and terms as legal/operational requirements evolve.
- Explicit legal disclaimer and mandatory legal-validation posture for templates.

**Residual risk**
- Country-level mandatory rules may override website-level framework language.

**Next enhancement**
- Maintain annual legal mapping against priority jurisdictions and publish a review log.

### 4) Intellectual Property Risk — Green

**Current controls**
- Company retention of frameworks, accelerators, and reusable assets.
- Strict limitation on implied license scope and duration.
- Prohibitions on reverse engineering and unauthorized extraction/reuse.

**Residual risk**
- Contract negotiation pressure for expanded downstream rights.

**Next enhancement**
- Prepare fallback IP language packs (standard, enterprise, strategic-tier).

### 5) Dispute / Litigation Risk — Amber

**Current controls**
- Contract-first governing law and dispute process model.
- Good-faith commercial resolution requirement before formal proceedings.
- Jurisdiction template library for major markets.

**Residual risk**
- Enforceability variance across countries and arbitration framework selection.

**Next enhancement**
- Pre-approve default dispute routes by region (court vs arbitration) with counsel.

## Priority Actions (Next 90 Days)

2. Align MSA/SOW templates with website legal baseline and liability language.
3. Establish compliance evidence trail (policy revision history, template governance, review approvals).
4. Create legal intake workflow for high-risk client redlines (IP, liability, data transfer, AI governance).

## Governance Notes

- This dashboard is an internal governance artifact and not legal advice.
- Final legal enforceability depends on executed agreements and applicable mandatory law.
- Review cadence should be at least annual, or earlier if legal/regulatory change is material.
